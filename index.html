Interactive Media Design Program

Learn about our program and how it prepares students for the industry.

Welcome to the Future of Digital Creativity Are you ready to embark on a journey into the exciting world of digital media design, development, and production? Look no further than our two-year diploma program in Interactive Media Design! At Fanshawe College, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that equips you with the skills, knowledge, and experience you need to thrive in today's dynamic digital landscape. Why Choose Interactive Media Design Program? In our Interactive Media Design program, we blend creativity with technology to prepare you for an exhilarating career in the digital media industry. Level 4 Client Project

In level four, student groups are tasked with working alongside non-profit organizations to put their skills to the test. The organizations will take the role of a client, and the students will create a final site for the organization, using all the skills they have learned throughout.
